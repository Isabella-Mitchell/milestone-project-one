About Row Gallery

Our History and Mission

Located in Dungeness, Row Gallery is an inclusive arts centre set in the heart of a unique coastal landscape.

Since its founding by John and Caroline Ackroyd in 1990, we have gained a reputation for our compelling exhibitions of local, national and international artists. Our fundamental belief is that by supporting emerging artists we will help them and the creative community here in Dungeness to thrive and grow.

Our team of friendly staff are on hand to answer enquiries and provide information about our artists and artworks. We offer a consultation service in person and on the phone.

The gallery atmosphere is open and welcoming to all visitors. All exhibitions are free admission and we are wheelchair accessible.

How To Get Here

Visit Row Gallery

By Car

From the M20, leave at junction 10 and follow A2070 towards Brenzett and then the A259 towards New Romney. Turn right onto B2075 towards Lydd. Follow signposts in Lydd to Dungeness.

By Train

The nearest train station is Rye (10 miles). You can then take the 102 bus to the gallery. Other train stations with buses to Dungeness include Ashford International (16 miles) and Folkestone (20 miles).

By Bike

Dungeness is on the National Cycle Route 2. There are cycling routes from Rye, Ashford International and Folkestone train stations.

Plan Your Visit

Where to Eat, Drink & Stay in Dungeness

Cafes & Restaurants

There are several cafes and pubs within walking distance of the gallery. The Britannia Inn and The End of the Line Restaurant are open daily for lunch. The Pilot Inn is open daily for lunch and dinner.

Accomodation

There are plenty of places to stay both in and near Dungeness. With options ranging from self-catering rentals to local hotels, and guest houses, there is something to suit everyone.

Local Attractions

Dungeness is a popular spot for lovers of nature and architecture alike. Attractions include the RSPB reserve, the New Lighthouse, Prospect Cottage and the area's many beaches.

Book a Consulation

Our free consultation service helps make the process of finding and buying art easier. Book a call or a meeting with one of our friendly team, and we will guide you through the process.
